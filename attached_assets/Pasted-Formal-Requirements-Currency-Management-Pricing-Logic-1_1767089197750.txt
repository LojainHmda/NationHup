Formal Requirements – Currency Management & Pricing Logic

1. Supported Currencies (Baseline)

The system shall support the following currencies by default:

ILS (Shekel)

USD (US Dollar)

EUR (Euro)

JOD (Jordanian Dinar)

GBP (British Pound)

2. Manual Currency Extension

The system shall allow manual addition of new currencies.

A currency can be added:

During product/collection upload, or

From the Admin Panel under currency management.

Any manually added currency must be configurable with exchange rates before being used in pricing.

3. Product & Collection Currency Assignment

Each product or collection must be associated with one base currency only.

The selected currency must be either:

One of the supported default currencies, or

A manually added currency.

Product prices shall be stored in their assigned base currency.

4. Customer Profile – Default Currency

When creating a Customer Profile, exactly one default display currency must be selected.

This currency determines how all product prices are displayed to the customer.

The customer shall be able to change the default currency from their profile settings.

5. Admin Panel – Exchange Rate Management

The Admin Panel shall act as the single source of truth for all exchange rates.

The admin must be able to:

Define and update exchange rates between all supported and manually added currencies.

No currency conversion shall occur without an admin-defined exchange rate.

6. Pricing & Conversion Logic

If the product currency matches the customer’s default currency:

The price is displayed as stored.

If the product currency differs from the customer’s default currency:

The system shall automatically convert the price using the admin-defined exchange rate.

The converted price shall be displayed in the customer’s selected currency.

7. Real-Time Price Reflection

Any update to exchange rates in the Admin Panel shall:

Immediately reflect across all customer-facing product prices.

Require no manual reconfiguration of products.

8. Customer-Facing Display Rules

Customers shall see prices only in their selected default currency.

No alternative currencies or raw base prices shall be exposed to the customer.

9. Business Outcome

Centralized currency control

Consistent pricing logic across the platform

Scalable and extensible currency architecture

Zero ambiguity between admin pricing and customer display
